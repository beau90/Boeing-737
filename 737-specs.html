Fuselage/Wings:

  • The Boeing 737 fuselage is 0.04 inches thick.
  • The length of the fuselage is 129 feet 6 inches.
  • The height of the fuselage is 41 feet 2 inches.
  • The Boeing 737's wingspan is 117 feet 10 inches.
  • The Boeing 737's wings are made of aluminum alloy. They are light in weight, strong, and relatively cheap.
  • In the center of the wing, the fuel tank can hold up to 4,299 gallons of fuel or 28,803 lbs.

Engines:

  • The CFM56-7B, designed by General Electric,is the most popular engine-aircraft combination in commercial aviation history. The Boeing 737 has two CFM56-7B engines.
  • Over 15,000 CFM56-7B engines have been delivered to power the B737 Aircraft.
  • The CFM56-7B has a thrust range of 19,500 to 27,300 pounds.
  • The fan blade are made of 22 solid titanium in a aluminum casing.
  • To power the B737, this engine burns up to 5,000lbs of fuel per hour.
  • The CFM56-7B engine has a fuel cost of $2,202 per hour.

Range:

  • The Boeing 737's range can vary. A typical 737 has a maximum range of 3,574 nautical miles or 4,112 land miles.
  • Factors such as passenger load, cargo load, wind conditions, and altitude can affect a Boeing 737's range.
  • With it's range capability, the Boeing 737 has an average of 25 routes over the Atlantic.
  • Most flights on a Boeing 737 last up to 7 hours.
  • The range without any operating engines can fly 60 to 100 miles.

Performance:

  • The Boeing 737 has a maximum speed of 538 miles per hour.
  • The Boeing 737 has a cruising speed of 514 miles per hour.
  • The Boeing 737 has max altitude of 41,000 feet, but 37,000 feet is optimal. This altitude can be reached in just 13 minutes.
  • The Boeing 737's wings design can help increase fuel capacity and efficiency.
  • Upon landing, the Boeing 737 touches down at a whopping 140 miles per hour.
Cabin Specifications:
  • The inside of the Boeing 737's cabin is 11 feet, 6 inches in width.
  • The inside of the Boeing 737's cabin is 7 feet in height.
  • The cabin can be arranges to carry 162 to 189 passengers.
  • The cabin can be configured in many ways, for example: A boardroom, lounge, VIP bedroom, and even a lavatory with showers.
  • It is equipped with large overhead bins, and LED lighting throughout.
